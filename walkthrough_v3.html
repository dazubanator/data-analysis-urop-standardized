Experiment Analysis V3 - Walkthrough

New in V3: Subject exclusion rule - subjects with more than 2 angle-invalidated trials are completely removed from the dataset.

Overview

Analysis V3 implements stricter quality control with subject-level exclusion:

  • Face ID Mapping: ID001 → ID017, ID022 → ID015
  • Angle Transformation: Raw angles multiplied by -1 (Left tilt) or 1 (Right tilt) to get end angles
  • Angle Validation: End angles must be strictly between 3 and 40 degrees
  • Subject Exclusion: Subjects with >2 angle-invalid trials are completely removed
  • Pairing Logic: Trials paired by Face Side (Face Left/Tilt Left vs Face Left/Tilt Right)
